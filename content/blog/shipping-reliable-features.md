Reliability is less about heroic fixes and more about small design decisions that prevent fire drills.

## Start with guardrails

Feature flags, rollbacks, and staged rollouts create a buffer between experimentation and production impact.

## Instrument early

Observability shouldn't be a phase; it should be part of the initial build. Logs, traces, and alerts make your future self faster.

## Close the loop

Every release should ship with a plan for validation. If the signals move, you can act quickly and confidently.
